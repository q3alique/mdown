# Getting Started with MDown

Welcome! This document is a quick tour of the everyday Markdown that MDown
renders in the live preview. Open it, then watch the right-hand pane update as
you scroll.

> **Tip:** Press `Ctrl+P` to jump between open files, and `Ctrl+,` to open
> settings.

## Headings keep your outline tidy

Every heading you write shows up in the **Outline** panel. Click a heading
there and both the editor and the preview jump to it.

## Text formatting

You can make text **bold**, *italic*, ***both***, ~~struck through~~, or
`inline code`. Combine them freely — for example, a **`bold code span`**.

Links are easy too: visit the [MDown repository](https://github.com/q3alique/mdown).
